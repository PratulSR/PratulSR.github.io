Privacy Policy for DogsHaven

Effective Date: 21 May 2023

Thank you for choosing DogsHaven! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and manage your personal information when you use our web information system to book day-care services for your dogs.

Information We Collect

  1. Personal Information

    When you use DogsHaven, we may collect personal information from you, such as your name, email address, phone number, and billing information. This information is necessary to provide our services and process your bookings.

  2. Dog Details

    To facilitate day-care bookings, we may collect information about your dogs, including their names, breeds, ages, vaccination records, and any special requirements they may have. This information helps us ensure the well-being and safety of your dogs during their stay.

  3. Usage Information

    We may collect non-personal information about your interactions with our system, such as the pages you visit, the actions you take, and the duration of your sessions. This data helps us improve our services and enhance your user experience.

Collection Methods

We collect information from you when you voluntarily provide it through our booking forms, registration process, or when you interact with our system. We may also collect information automatically through cookies and similar tracking technologies. By using DogsHaven, you consent to the use of cookies as described in our Cookie Policy.

Purpose of Data Collection

We collect and use your personal information for the following purposes:

  1. Booking Services

    To process your day-care bookings, communicate with you about your reservations, and provide the requested services.

  2. Communication

    To send you important updates, confirmations, or notifications related to your bookings, as well as respond to your inquiries or customer support requests.

  3. Improving Services

    To analyze usage patterns, gather feedback, and enhance our system's functionality, user interface, and overall user experience.

  4. Marketing

    With your consent, we may use your contact information to send you promotional materials, newsletters, or offers that may be of interest to you. You can opt-out of receiving such communications at any time.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our compliance with Australian privacy laws, or if you wish to exercise your privacy rights, you can contact our Privacy Officer using the details provided below.
